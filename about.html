I am a driven, motivated, and curious software engineer who is fascinated at the impact technology has on communities around me. I enjoy working on projects that give me the opportunity to see the effect my work has on its users. I am a senior at Rice University studying computer science and minoring in data science, seeking full-time, new grad software engineering opportunities to analyze and solve complex problems by using technology and collaborating with others. I was a software engineering intern at Apple working on refactoring pipelines this summer. I have previously interned at Facebook doing web development. I serve as the internal vice chair for the Asian Pacific American Student Alliance on campus where I help oversee the organization and plan events. I am also an active member of CSters, the women in computer science club at Rice.
